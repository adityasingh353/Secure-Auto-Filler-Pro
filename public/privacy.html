Privacy Policy for Secure Auto-Filler Pro

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

Introduction

Secure Auto-Filler Pro is created to help users fill online examination and registration forms more efficiently. Protecting user privacy is one of the primary goals of this extension.

By installing and using Secure Auto-Filler Pro, you acknowledge and agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection

Secure Auto-Filler Pro does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share any personal information with the developer or any third party.

The extension operates entirely on the user's device and does not use any external servers, cloud storage, analytics services, telemetry, or tracking mechanisms.

Local Storage

Information entered by the user, including personal details and uploaded documents, is stored only on the user's own device using browser storage mechanisms.

No user data is accessible to the developer.

Data Security

Data saved by the extension is encrypted before storage. Access to encrypted information depends on the Master Password created by the user.

The developer does not possess, store, or have access to the user's Master Password or decrypted data.

User Responsibility

Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their devices and protecting their Master Password.

Because the developer has no access to user data, the developer cannot recover lost passwords, restore deleted information, or retrieve encrypted data.

Users acknowledge that device compromise, malware infections, unauthorized access to their own devices, forgotten passwords, or other events beyond the developer's control may result in loss of access to stored information.

Third-Party Services

Secure Auto-Filler Pro does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external databases.

Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed toward children under the age required by applicable law, and no information is knowingly collected from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the extension after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, users may contact the developer through the contact information provided on the Chrome Web Store listing.

By installing and using Secure Auto-Filler Pro, users acknowledge that all information stored by the extension remains under their own control and responsibility, and that the developer has no ability to access, recover, or monitor user data.
